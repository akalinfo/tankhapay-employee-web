FAQs

1. How can Tankha Pay help with creating salary structures?
-The software calculates the CTC based on the in hand salary you provide. It takes into account the minimum wages, PF/ESI compliance and the various components of salary structuring.
2.Is the payroll software compliant with legal regulations?
-TankhaPay is designed to comply with all regulations, and is regularly updated based on any changes in the compliances.
3. How can I add new employees to the payroll system?
-Employees can be added to TankhaPay either individually or in bulk. To add the employees individually, click on add employee and simply add their full name, phone number and email address (optional). You can also choose to send them the link to download the TankhaPay app. To add employees in bulk, click on bulk employees under add employees, and download the excel template. As per the instructions provided, add employees to the excel and upload the same. The employees would need to complete onboarding on the TankhaPay app which includes Profile updation, KYC verification, and bank account verification, before you can setup their salaries. Alternatively, you could complete these steps on the behalf of the employee through the Tankha Pay business login.
4. What are the 3 modes in Payrolling?
Self: Use our system to calculate your payroll and maintain attendance records. File compliance and process salary payments yourself.

Comply Serve: Streamline your payroll processes, effortlessly track attendance, and let us handle the complexities of compliance. Experience the convenience of disbursing salaries yourself.

Pay Serve: Optimize your payroll process, track attendance without hassle, and maintain statutory compliance. Let us handle the complexities of compliances and easily process salary payments through our platform.
